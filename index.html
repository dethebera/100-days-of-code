‚úÖ Day 91 - 100 Company-specific

Now, First of all! .... Congrats on reaching day 91! This must have been tiring and hectic but then "no pain no gain" right.

Company-specific is exactly what it means, one takes these challenges to up-skill themselves and crack coding interviews to get placed in their dream companies, but do remember the person hiring you also did the same thing.

Companies nowadays know that there are resources available online that can get you to master coding rounds along with other various rounds. So what isn't everyone who clears the initial rounds clear personal interview? The answer is a cultural fit, every company has their own culture that they follow, certain principles they do not fail to highlight, understand the company culture. Network, connect and interact with those already in that company to understand how it works.

Another major letdown can be if the person is "faking it". Now, everyone wants to get placed in good companies to get a higher salary ( especially for the ones just starting ). But, this mentality can hamper the company's growth because the employee would just be looking at the ways of surviving the month rather can contributing to the growth. Companies have recruiters who have aced psychology and are sitting there to catch the candidates faking it.

To sum it up -

‚úÖ Understand company culture.

‚úÖ Tailor resum√© according to company's requirements.

‚úÖ Understand the role you are filling.

‚úÖ Work on your leadership skills.

‚úÖ Pinpoint on how you will contribute to the company.

You have it in you folks! Get out there and crush it.

Always remember, the interviewer was also interviewed once. Person to person is the best way to keep it real.
